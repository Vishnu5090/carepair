Job Opportunities for Differently-Abled People

Job Recommendations

Explore personalized job recommendations based on your skills and abilities:

  • GetAbilities: Tailored jobs for individuals with disabilities based on personal skills and interests.
  • DisabledPerson: Provides employment opportunities for differently-abled people across various industries.

Job Portals for Disabled People

Find specific job portals focused on providing opportunities to differently-abled individuals:

  • AbilityJobs: The largest job board for people with disabilities, offering a wide range of job listings.
  • Braille Works: Job listings and career advice specifically for individuals with visual impairments.

Resources for Skill Development

Access free training and resources to improve your skill set and prepare for job opportunities:

  • Udemy: Offers thousands of courses to help you develop new skills.
  • Coursera: Courses on business, tech, and more, including many free options.

Disability-Friendly Employers

Browse companies that actively promote diversity and inclusion in their workforce:

  • Indeed: A collection of job listings from disability-friendly employers.
  • Walmart Careers: Offers a variety of positions with accommodations for people with disabilities.
